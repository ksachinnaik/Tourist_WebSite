About Travel Assistant

Founded in 2021 in Hyderabad, Booking.com has grown from a small indian start-up to one of the world’s leading digital travel companies. Travel Assistant’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the world.

By investing in technology that takes the friction out of travel, Travel Assistant seamlessly connects millions of travelers to memorable experiences, a variety of transportation options, and incredible places to stay – from homes to hotels, and much more. As one of the world’s largest travel marketplaces for both established brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, Travel Assistant enables properties around the world to reach a global audience and grow their businesses.

Travel Assistant is available in 43 languages and offers more than 28 million reported accommodation listings, including over 6.2 million homes, apartments, and other unique places to stay. Wherever you want to go and whatever you want to do, Travel Assistant makes it easy and supports you with 24/7 customer support.
