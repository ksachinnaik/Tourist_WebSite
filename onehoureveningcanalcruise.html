This cruise through Amsterdam’s canals will give you the chance to see some of the city’s iconic landmarks and learn about its culture and history. After departing from the Anne Frank House, your cruise tour will pass by iconic spots like the Jordaan neighbourhood, the Magere Brug, the Golden Ben and many more landmarks. Along the way, the boat crew will offer local insights into the city’s history and some of its hidden gems. With a small group of people on board, you'll have plenty of opportunities to ask questions, get recommendations and enjoy the sight of Amsterdam’s bridges lighting up at night
