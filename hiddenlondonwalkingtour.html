Instant Conformation

On this tour, you’ll head off the beaten track with a local guide to see a side of London known only to a select few. You’ll spot hidden medieval courtyards and other typically overlooked locations as your guide tells you intriguing tales of the past. Around Fleet Street and Temple, you’ll discover a labyrinth of lanes and explore historic cloisters, forgotten churches and tucked-away rivers. You’ll also learn about some of the more secret elements of the city’s past, like the places where the Knights Templar once worshipped.
