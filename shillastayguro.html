Shilla Stay Guro

Open from February 2016, Shilla Stay Guro is conveniently located a 5-minute walk from Exit 6 of Guro Digital Complex Subway Station on Line 2. This Samsung Group's hotel offers easy access to popular neighbourhoods including Hongdae, Sindorim, COEX and Euljiro 1-ga/Myeongdong within a 30-minute subway ride. This modern business hotel houses a restaurant, bar, fitness centre and offers free WiFi throughout the property. Elegantly furnished in neutral colours, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and minibar. The en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer. The 24-hour front desk offers free luggage storage, free newspaper service and currency exchange. Concierge service along with ticketing and tour services are also available upon request. There is a business centre and meeting rooms at the Guro Shilla Stay.
